What Is Contract-to-Hire Staffing ?

Contract-to-Hire is a strategic recruitment model where a candidate works on a contract basis for a trial period with the specific intent of potentially becoming a permanent employee. During this phase, the professional remains on Greenwave Staffing's Inc payroll while performing as a full member of your team16. This model is ideal for organizations that want to eliminate hiring mistakes and ensure a perfect long-term fit1more_horiz.

Our Proven 4-Step Contract-to-Hire Process

Understand Project Requirements

We begin by deeply analyzing your workforce needs, project scope, and the specific cultural fit required for permanent success.

Source & Screen Talent

Our recruiters tap into a global talent network to rigorously assess and background-check candidates specifically for long-term potential.

Curated Shortlist Presentation

We present a curated shortlist of pre-vetted candidates within 48–72 hours, including full profiles and interview notes.

Seamless Onboarding & Trial

We handle all paperwork, compliance, and payroll during the contract period so your new professional can focus on delivering results.
