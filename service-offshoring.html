What Is Offshore Staffing?

Offshore Staffing is a strategic recruitment solution where Greenwave Staffing Inc helps you build and manage teams located in different geographical regions, such as India, to support your domestic operations. Unlike traditional outsourcing, these professionals act as an extension of your local team. We handle the entire recruitment lifecycle—from global sourcing to local compliance and payroll—allowing you to maintain high standards of quality while benefiting from global cost efficiencies.

Specialized Talent Solutions Across Borders

  • IT & Technology Excellence: Access expert software Developers, Architects, AI Engineers, AI Developers, Machine Learning, Data Analyst, and Data Scientists from top global tech hubs.
  • Non-IT Support: Secure high-quality talent for finance, accounting, HR, administrative operations and many more.
  • India Domestic Staffing: Leverage our deep roots in India to find qualified professionals for permanent, contract, and project-based roles across multiple sectors.
  • Rapid Deployment: Leverage our pre-screened talent pool for immediate hiring needs.

Our Proven 4-Step Global Hiring Process

Define Global Requirements

We analyze your technical needs and timezone requirements to determine the best offshore strategy for your culture.

Global Source & Screen

Our recruiters tap into an extensive international network to rigorously assess, background-check, and verify the credentials of every candidate.

Curated Shortlist Presentation

Within 48–72 hours, you receive a shortlist of top-tier global matches with detailed profiles and interview notes.

Compliant Onboarding

We manage all international paperwork, local labor laws, and payroll so your offshore team is productive from day one.
