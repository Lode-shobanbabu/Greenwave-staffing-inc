What Is Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO)?

RPO is a specialized workforce solution where a company transfers all or part of its permanent recruitment and payroll functions to an external provider like Greenwave Staffing Inc. Unlike traditional headhunting, our RPO service provides a dedicated infrastructure, specialized technology, and expert recruiters who manage every touchpoint of the candidate journey under your brand. We deliver a seamless, scalable hiring engine that reduces cost-per-hire and significantly improves talent quality.

Specialized Talent Solutions Across Borders

  • Managed Talent Sourcing: We utilize advanced global networks to find exactly the right candidates for any role.
  • Full Lifecycle Recruitment: We manage job postings, resume screening, initial interviews, and background checks.
  • Compliance & Payroll Support: We ensure all hiring is fully compliant with local regulations while managing 100% of the payroll administration.
  • Employer Brand Enhancement: Our recruiters act as your brand ambassadors, ensuring a professional and positive candidate experience.

Our Proven 4-Step RPO Process

Strategic Alignment

We begin by understanding your business goals, compliance requirements, skill gaps, and long-term culture fit to design the right hiring strategy.

Global Talent Acquisition

Our recruiters leverage a vast international network to source, screen, and rigorously assess candidates through verification and background checks.

Curated Shortlist & Selection

Within 48–72 hours, we deliver a curated shortlist of pre-vetted professionals along with insights and expert recommendations for faster hiring decisions.

Integration & Ongoing Management

We handle onboarding, compliance, and payroll support while staying engaged post-placement to ensure smooth integration and long-term retention.
