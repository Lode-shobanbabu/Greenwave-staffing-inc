What Is Direct Hire Staffing?

Direct Hire Staffing is our premier recruitment model focused on filling permanent, full-time positions within your organization. Unlike temporary or contract models, direct hire candidates become your employees from day one. At Greenwave Staffing Inc, we handle the entire heavy lifting—from sourcing and screening to final negotiations—allowing you to focus on your core business while we secure the talent that will drive your future success.

Comprehensive Talent Solutions Across Key Departments

  • IT Professionals: Evaluate expert Developers, Architects, AI Engineers, AI Developers, Machine Learning, Data Scientist, Data Analysts in a real-world environment.
  • Administrative & Support : Finding the reliable backbone of your daily operations.
  • Specialized Sectors : Dedicated hiring for Healthcare, Finance, Manufacturing, Logistics, Constructions, Civil Engineering, Pharma and across all the industries.
  • Executive & Leadership : Securing visionary leaders for C-suite and management roles.

Our Proven 4-Step Direct Hiring Process

Understand Your Culture

We deep-dive into your industry's compliance needs, skill gaps, and specific company culture to define the "perfect fit".

Strategic Sourcing

Our recruiters tap into a global talent network across the USA, UK, and Canada to find active and passive candidates.

Rigorous Screening

Every candidate undergoes meticulous evaluation, including skills assessments and background checks, before they ever reach your desk.

Selection & Onboarding

We present a curated shortlist of pre-vetted candidates within 48–72 hours, managing the offer and onboarding logistics for a seamless transition.

Key Features & Benefits of Permanent Placement

  • Reduced Employee Turnover: Our focus on technical expertise and cultural alignment helps improve long-term employee retention.
  • Access to Hidden Talent: We connect you with highly skilled professionals who may not be actively searching but are ideal for your organization.
  • Industry-Specific Recruiters: Our specialized recruiters understand the unique demands, skills, and compliance requirements of your industry.
  • Cost-Effective Recruitment: Reduce advertising expenses and save valuable internal hiring time by receiving pre-screened qualified candidates.
  • 98% Client Satisfaction: Businesses across multiple industries trust Greenwave Staffing Inc for delivering reliable and high-quality hiring solutions.
